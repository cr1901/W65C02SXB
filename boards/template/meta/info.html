W65C02SXB Expansion Board

Expansion Board Template For 6502 Devlopment

The W65C02SXB is a 65C02 development board by Western Design Center that allows users to evaluate the 65C02 microprocessor and associated peripherals.

This expansion board template breaks out:

  • 40-pin "XBUS_02" connector consisting of 6502 D0-D7, A0-A15, and control lines.
  • 6521 PIA ports PA0-PA7, CA1, CA2, PB0-PB7, CB1, and CB2.
  • 6522 VIA ports PA0-PA7, CA1, CA2, PB0-PB7, CB1, and CB2.
  • 6551 ACIA ports RTSB, DTRB, RXD, DSRB, TXD, DCDB, CTSB, and ACIA_RXC.

Right now, only the W65C02SXB board is supported, as the XBUS_02 and the XBUS_816 on the W65C816SXB are not quite compatible. However, adding support for the latter should not be difficult.

Special thanks goes to Western Design Center for providing me mechanical drawings and Gerbers so that I could make this template.
